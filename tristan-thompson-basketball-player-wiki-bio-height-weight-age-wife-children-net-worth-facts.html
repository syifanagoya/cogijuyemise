Tristan Thompson (Basketball Player) Wiki, Bio, Height, Weight, Age, Wife, Children, Net Worth, Fact

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Who is Tristan Thompson? He is a Canadian professional basketball player. He is better known as NBA forward known for his rebounding ability. He was selected 4th overall by the Cleveland Cavaliers in 2011 and he helped the team win the NBA Championship in 2016. Tune in bio!

Tristan Thompson Height & Weight

How tall is Tristan Thompson? He stands at a height of 6 ft 9 in tall or else 2.06 m or 206 cm. He weighs around 115 Kg or 254 lbs. He is a gymnast and fitness freak as well. He has black eyes and black hair.

Tristan Thompson Wife

Who is the wife of Tristan Thompson? He had a son named Prince with ex-girlfriend and model Jordan Craig. In 2016, he began dating Khloe Kardashian. He and Khloe welcomed a daughter named True together in April 2018. In early 2019, he and Khloe separated.

Facts about Tristan Thompson

  • He was born on March 13, 1991 in Toronto, Canada. At present, he is 29-yrs old. He holds Canadian nationality and belongs to mixed ethnicity.
  • He played one year of college basketball for Texas before declaring for the 2011 NBA Draft.
  • As of his educational qualifications, he played high school basketball at three different schools: St. Marguerite d’Youville Secondary School in Ontario, Saint Benedict’s Preparatory School in New Jersey, and Findlay Prep in Nevada.
  • In 2011, he was drafted along with point guard Kyrie Irving by the Cleveland Cavaliers.
  • In 2008, Thompson represented his country and Canada Basketball at the FIBA Americas Under-18 Championship.
  • In the same year, he played one game in a qualifying tournament for the 2019 FIBA World Cup.
  • He has also played for the Canadian national team.
  • Thompson won an NBA championship with the Cavaliers in 2016.
  • He played one season of college basketball for the Texas Longhorns before being drafted fourth overall by the Cavaliers in the 2011 NBA draft.
