The Good, the Bad and the Funny.

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

In the world of fashion, its rare that somebody doesn't know the name of Kardishian sisters. Majorly the Kim who got media attention as a friend and stylist of Paris Hilton.

Talking about her fashion sense, she is the fashion queen of America. Kim Kardishian is the popular name in the fashion world.

Fashion journey of Kardashian

Kim was born on October 21, 1980, in Los Angeles California. She is the daughter of Robert and Kris Kardashian.

Though she was a sibling of famous models Kylie and Kendall, she was not more known to the public like them. But when her sexual exploits video was leaked online with her boyfriend she was renowned.

Fashion Icon 2021

Talking about her outfit she is always a queen for the fashion sense. She was awarded the Fashion Icon honor in 2021 and people's choice awards on December 7.

Kardashian's good outfit

Kim never fails to amaze people with her fashion sense. She is always an icon for the fashion world.

Talking about her good outfits, all outfits to the date are good and amazing and iconic.

Met Gala 

The winner of fashion icon 2021's good and iconic outfit till yet the iconic dress is the dress worn by her at famous fashion show Met Gala 2021. 

She wore a dress designed by Thierry with the help of the world's foremost most corset maker Mugler that resembles water droplets from the neck to the knee which was the maroon color.

Worst outfit of Kardashian

The worst outfit of the winner of people's choice award till yet is the black dress from head to toe. 

The outfit is called Balenciaga and the designer was Demna Gvasalia. She wore the dress on the red carpet of the Met Gala 2021.

Funny outfits of Kim

The winner of Fashion icon 2021's funny outfit till yet is a Newspaper printed skirt worn at the NYC Levi's event in Los Angeles.

She wore a white top in the upper waist and a Newspaper print skirt designed by John Galliano.
