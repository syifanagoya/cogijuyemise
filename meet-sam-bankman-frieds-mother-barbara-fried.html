Barbara Fried Son: Sam Bankman-Fried

Sam Bankman-Fried is a prominent American businessman and entrepreneur who has made a name for himself in the financial technology industry. He is best known as the founder, CEO, and president of Alameda Research, one of the world’s leading crypto trading firms. Sam also founded FTX, the popular cryptocurrency derivatives exchange, and serves as its CEO.

Sam began his career in finance and investing while attending Stanford University, where he received a Bachelor’s degree in mathematics. After graduation, he worked as an analyst at Silicon Valley investment firm Greylock Partners before joining Jane Street Capital in New York City.

Barbara Fried Husband

Barbara Fried is married with Joseph Bankman, fellow Stanford law professor. Also, Barbara and Joseph Bankman are signatories to a Bahama residence that is part of the FTX bankruptcy.

Fried’s sister Linda P. Fried is the Dean of the Columbia University’s Mailman School of Public Health.
