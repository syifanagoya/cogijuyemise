Celebrity Weddings of 2022: 15 Stars Got Married

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 20, 2024

We saw many celebrity weddings of 2022 after the covid pandemic. Some celebrity weddings of 2022 are Gabourey Sidibe and Brandon Frankel, and Porsha Williams and Simon Guobadia.

As such, people have started celebrating life in the best way possible - by getting married. And celebrities are not any different.

From secret and private wedding ceremonies to all-out extravaganzas, many celebrity couples decided to tie the knot this year. Be it superstars or reality stars, many stars were feeling the love in the air after two long years of being unable to breathe.

So, in order to celebrate love in an informative way, here is a list of fifteen celebrity weddings of 2022. Let's explore famous stars who got married recently and see if they are still together now that the year is getting ready to close.

Gabourey Sidibe And Brandon Frankel

Gabourey Sidibe revealed that she got married on March 2021 to her husband Brandon Frankel on a December 5 appearance on the talk show, Live With Kelly and Ryan. 

In the appearance, she mentioned that she and Frankel had gotten engaged in November 2020 and had wanted to get married much earlier, but because of Covid, they couldn't. They eventually had a small ceremony with only family and friends in March 2021.

Soon after she revealed the news in the interview, Frankel posted a picture of them on his Instagram, showing off their wedding rings, with the caption, "I can finally call her my WIFE publicly."

Gabourey first gained popularity for her critically acclaimed starring role in the 2009 movie "Precious" and, since then, has had a string of prominent roles in various movies and television shows. On the other hand, Frankel works as a talent manager with Cameo. 

Porsha Williams And Simon Guobadia

Porsha Williams got engaged to her current husband, Simon Guobadia, in May 2021 after only dating for one month.

After over a year of being engaged, they got married on November 25, 2022. They first had a traditional Nigerian wedding per Guobadia's cultural heritage on November 2, 2022, and then a traditional American wedding on November 26, 2022.

Porsha Williams is a television personality who is best known for starring in the reality television series The Real Housewives of Atlanta from 2012 until 2021 and has since started starring in her own spin-off series, Porsha's Family Matters.

She was married to former American Football Quarterback Kordell Stweart from 2011 to 2013. Her current husband, Simon, is a Nigerian-American entrepreneur.

Madison LeCroy And Brett Randle

Madison LeCroy tied the knot with her husband Brett Randle, in a romantic Mexican wedding surrounded by loved ones. 

The wedding took place on November 19 on a Saturday. However, they had officially exchanged their vows in an intimate civil ceremony three days earlier on Wednesday, November 16. They got on a flight immediately the next day as LeCroy shared a picture of her new husband on a plane. 

Madison LeCroy is best known for being in the American reality television series Southern Charm. She started dating her now husband in April 2021. They met at a bar in Scottsdale, Arizona, while she was there for a bachelorette party, and he was there for a friend's birthday party.

They went public with their relationship in June 2021 and got engaged in October 2021. Before marrying Randle, LeCroy married Josh Hughes, with whom she shares a 9-year-old son. 

Taylor Lautner And Taylor Dome

American Actor Taylor Lautner set the internet on fire when he married his longtime girlfriend Taylor Dome on November 11, 2022. 

The marriage came a year after he proposed to her. The marriage took everyone's breath away due to Taylor Lautner's past relationship with Taylor Swift.

During this time, a meme started circulating stating that if Taylor Lautner and Taylor Swift married, Taylor Swift would become Taylor Lautner. It was a harmless joke, and when their relationship quietly withered away, no one overthought of it. 

But now, over a decade later, Taylor Lautner married someone named Taylor, bringing that meme back to life and making their marriage the Twitter talk for a solid couple of days. 

Tiffany Trump And Michael Boulos

Tiffany Trump, the youngest daughter of disgraced former American president Donald Trump, married businessman Michael Boulos on November 12, 2022. 

Tiffany is the sole daughter of Trump's second wife, Marla Ann Maples. She and Boulos exchanged vows at the Mar-a-Lago resort in Palm Beach, Florida. Donald Trump owns the resort. 

Tiffany and Michael Boulos have been dating since 2018. He is a Lebanese-American billionaire heir and business executive. His family owns the Boulos Enterprise, a Nigerian distribution assembly, and SCOA Nigeria in Nigeria.

The two met in the Summer of 2018 when Tiffany Trump was vacationing in Greece with actress Lindsay Lohan.

Michael Oher And Tiffany Roy

Michael Oher married his longtime lover Tifanny Roy on November 8, 2022. He shared the event on his Instagram for the world to see.

In a caption, he wrote that he was filled with joy and happiness and that people took the time to come and celebrate his bond with his beautiful wife. 

He is a former National Football League player who played for eight seasons for the Baltimore Ravens. However, he is most popular for inspiring the popular 2006 book The Blind Side: Evolution of a Game by Michael Lewis.

The book inspired the Oscar-winning 2009 film The Blind Side starring Sandra Bullock. 

He met his wife Tiffany Roy, while they were both studying at the University of Mississippi. They have since had four children: two sons and two daughters, and got engaged on July 21, 2021. 

When they got married, they had already been together for 17 years. 

Ronen Rubinstein And Jessica Parker Kennedy

Ronen Rubenstein finally spilled the beans about his marital status on November 2 in an interview with Hello! Magazine. 

He stated that he had married his longtime girlfriend, Jessica Parker Kennedy, three months earlier. The two had gotten married in a small farm wedding in Canada, and it was a traditional Jewish ceremony. 

Ronen is an actor and singer best known for his roles as T.K Strand in 9-1-1: Lone Star and Matt Webb in the American Horror Stories series. He first gained prominence for his role as Nathan in the Netflix series Orange Is The New Back.

On the other hand, Kennedy is known for her roles in various television shows like The Secret Circle, Smallville, and The Flash. The two started dating in 2017 and got engaged in Portofino, Italy, two months before they married on August 14, 2022. 

Madison Prewett And Grant Michael Troutt

Madison Prewett married her now-husband, Grant Michael Troutt, on October 29 after dating for less than a year.

Prewett, best known for being the runner-up in the 24th season of The Bachelor, was popular for being adamant about dating for marriage. She gained national recognition when the Bachelor of the show, Peter Weber, broke up with the official winner Hannah Ann Sluss so he could be with Prewett. 

However, in an almost cruelly comedic twist of turns, they broke up after three days of dating. She released a book about her endeavors called "Made For This Moment" on October 19, 2021.

In another strange twist of turns, she met her current husband, Grant around this time and started dating him. They married in Dallas, Texas, in an extravagant traditional ceremony with their friends and family.

Astrid Loch And Kevin Wendt

Like the previous entry, Astrid Loch and Kevin Wendt are alumni of the Bachelor franchise.

Astrid Loch is a contestant from the 21 seasons of the Bachelor, while Kevin Wendt is a contestant and winner from the first season of The Bachelorette Canada. Though Wendt won the show and the heart of The Bachelorette, Jasmine Lorimer, the two split five months after the show ended.

Due to both of them being single, Loch and Wendt appeared on the fifth season of Bachelor in Paradise, which is a spin-off series where failed contestants from the Bachelor and Bachelorette can come for another shot at love.

Loch and Wendt instantly had a connection in the show but ended their relationship while the show was running. However, after the end of the show, they reconnected and started dating. 

They got engaged on August 28, 2019, and had their first child, a son, on November 20, 2021, and married on October 28, 2022.

Joe Amabile And Serena Pitt

Another Bachelor in Paradise alumni, Joe Amabile and Serena Pitt revealed on October 27 that they had an intimate wedding ceremony earlier that day.

The ceremony took place at a courthouse in New York City. According to their statement, they knew they wanted to get married as soon as they met, and though they are planning a big wedding next fall, they wanted to have a private ceremony because their lives are so public.

Joe Amabile is famous for both being a contestant on the 14th season of The Bachelorette and being eliminated in the first week. On the other hand, Serena Pitt was a contestant on the 25th season of the Bachelor.

The two met during the event season of Bachelor in Paradise and hit it off. And not only did they hit it off, but they got engaged at the end of the show and so won. Their official wedding ceremony will take place on September 2023. 

Lucas Adams And Shelby Wulfert

Lucas Adams and Shelby Wulfert married on October 15 in Waxahachie, Texas. 

Lucas is an American actor who is best known for his appearances in Disney's Liv and Maddie and True Blood. However, he gained fame and relevance when he started appearing in the long-running soap opera Days of Our Lives in 2017.

Since then, he has been nominated for a Daytime Emmy Award twice for his work in Days of Our Lives in the Outstanding Younger Actor category.

He met his now-wife Shelby Wulfert when they were both filming for Liv and Maddie in October 2015. They started dating shortly thereafter and have been together since. The couple got engaged in July 2021 and married on October 15, 2022. Their wedding took place at the historic Chateau Hiddenwood estate in Waxahachie, Texas. 

Lesley Murphy And Alex Kavanagh

The Bachelor alumni Lesley Murphy married Alex Kavanagh in Hawaii on October 14.

Murphy started gaining prominence as a contestant on the 17th season of The Bachelor and being the co-runner-up of the Bachelor Winter Games.

She started dating Alex Kavanagh while still with Dean Unglert, another Bachelor alum, whom she met at the Bachelor Winter Games. The two were very serious but broke up in early April 2018.

She had already started dating Alex Kavanagh in late 2017, months before she broke up with Unglert, and the two got engaged on February 4, 2020. They announced they were expecting their first child on September 27, 2020, and the next month, on October 17, they revealed it would be a girl. 

Their daughter was born on February 12, 2021, and after two years of being engaged, they married on October 14, 2022.

James Gunn And Jennifer Holland

James Gunn married his longtime girlfriend, Jennifer Holland, on September 30, 2022.

The two had been dating for over seven years and had even worked together in the DC television series Peacemaker, which Gunn directed. 

James revealed in an Instagram post that they had engaged in February after he proposed to her. Many stars from Gunn's other projects, like Guardians of the Galaxy, Peacemaker, and Suicide Squad, came to the wedding.

Gunn is famous as a writer and director behind many popular Marvel and DC movies and shows. Jennifer Holland is a popular actress who plays one of the main roles in Gunn's show Peacemaker alongside John Cena.

AnnaSophia Robb And Trevor Paul

Famous actress AnnaSophia Rob announced on September 12 that she married her long-time boyfriend, Trevor Paul.

AnnaSophia announced via Instagram and shared various pictures of their weddings. The first picture, which showed them walking down the aisle, was captioned "Just the beginning."

The wedding took place outdoors and was filled with AnnaSophia Robb and Trevor Paul's closest friends and family. AnnaSophia was filled with smiles during the whole ordeal. 

The wedding occurred earlier in September, but she hasn't revealed the date.  AnnaSophia Robb is best known for her many roles in such iconic movies as Twilight. However, her most famous role is as the titular character in The Carrie Diaries. 

Sarah Hyland And Wells Adams

Perhaps the most popular celebrity wedding of the year, Modern Family star Sarah Hyland and Bachelor alum Wells Adams finally tied the knot.

After three years of being engaged, the two finally married at a California vineyard in August 2020. The two had gotten engaged in July 2019 but had to postpone their wedding multiple times.

Their original time was in August 2020, but it was delayed due to the pandemic. When they finally married, various Modern Family alumni joined their wedding and even officiated by Jesse Tyler Ferguson, who played her uncle in the show.
