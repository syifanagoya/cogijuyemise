Southern Charm Season 8 Cast

Season 8 will include the return of Leva Bonaparte, Craig Conover, Kathryn Dennis, Austen Kroll, Madison LeCroy, and Shep Rose. This season, Olivia Flowers, Marcie Hobbs, Chleb Ravenell, and familiar characters Venita Aspen, Taylor Ann Green, and Naomie Olindo will join them.

Southern Charm 8 Expected Plot

For the first time ever, the notoriously closed society of Charleston, South Carolina, opens the doors of their centuries-old plantation homes, providing a rare inside look at the lives of modern-day Southern aristocracy. Get enchanted by the city’s social scene, which is bound by tradition and ostentation in a way that no other culture in America can match, thanks to a group of the city’s most charismatic gentlemen and their Southern belle counterparts.

Explore the world of exclusivity, money, and scandal that exists behind the walls of Charleston, South Carolina’s most aristocratic families and you will uncover a world that dates back generations. Following a group of Charleston singles, as they struggle to find their way in this tightly knit, posh society, the fast-paced, drama-filled docu-series follows them.
