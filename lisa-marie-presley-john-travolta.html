Lisa Marie Presley Had Close Relationship with John Travolta

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, March 27, 2024

Lisa Marie Presley, the daughter of the legendary Elvis Presley and his wife Priscilla Presley, formed a remarkably close relationship with fellow actor John Travolta. 

Their friendship transcended the glitz and glamour of Hollywood, as they found solace and support in each other during both joyous and challenging times. 

Despite the age difference and early rumors, their connection remained steadfast, rooted in mutual respect and understanding.

Through their unwavering support for one another, Lisa and Travolta proved that true friendship knows no bounds and can flourish in even the most extraordinary circumstances.

How did Lisa Marie Presley and John Travolta meet?

Following the untimely death of Lisa’s father, Elvis, Priscilla sought to bring some joy into her daughter’s life and utilized her connections in Hollywood to arrange a meeting between Lisa and Travolta.

Their initial encounter took place when Lisa was just 11 years old, while Travolta was in his mid-twenties. However, any speculations of a romantic relationship between the two are false.

Lisa Marie Presley. (Source: lisampresley/Instagram)

Nonetheless, an apparent connection between Priscilla and Travolta was evident during this time, leading to a few secretive outings together but reportedly did not go any further.

During their time together, Travolta took the opportunity to discuss his involvement in the Church of Scientology with Priscilla. Having been a member since 1975, he extended an invitation for her to explore the teachings and practices of Scientology for herself. 

Priscilla, finding the experience to be spiritually welcoming, decided to join the Church in 1977. Consequently, Lisa became affiliated with the institution as well.

Lisa Marie Presley supported John Travolta after the death of his son Jett

In the aftermath of the heartbreaking loss of Travolta’s son, Jett, Lisa stepped forward as a staunch supporter for her devastated friends. 

Jett, the eldest child of Travolta and Preston, had been afflicted by seizures from a young age and was later diagnosed with Kawasaki syndrome and autism. 

During a family trip to the Bahamas, Jett suffered a severe seizure while bathing, resulting in a devastating fall that claimed his life in 2009. 

John Travolta and his late wife Kelly Preston. (Source: Twitter)

Instead of receiving sympathy, Travolta and his late wife, Kelly Preston, found themselves wrongly blamed for their son’s tragic death, as baseless rumors spread that their involvement in Scientology was somehow responsible. 

When Jett passed away, the ambulance driver who had taken him to the hospital made a threat to spread the lie to the media that Travolta and Preston had refused to give Jett life-saving medical attention. 

The ambulance driver attempted to blackmail Travolta for $25 million to prevent this falsehood from being revealed.

Travolta and Preston refused the suggestion and sued him. He chose not to proceed with a new trial after a mistrial was declared as a result of a politician claiming to know the verdict before it was reached.

During the trial, it was clear what information the ambulance driver planned to disclose to the media, leading others to wonder if medical care was allowed in Scientology.

As a result, Travolta and Preston soon found themselves raising a child as members of a cult that forbade the use of modern medicine to treat Jett in the Bahamas.

Lisa, witnessing the onslaught of misinformation and the toll it took on her friends, felt compelled to speak out and set the record straight regarding Scientology’s beliefs regarding modern medicine. 

Taking to her MySpace blog, Presley referred to Travolta and Preston as “the sweetest people in the world” and expressed her deep empathy for the pain they were enduring.

Presley’s statement aimed to dispel the misconceptions surrounding Scientology, asserting that religion does not discourage medical care, medicine, or the involvement of medical professionals, regardless of the circumstances. 

She said, “Whatever medical and or physical condition Jett had, I can tell you first hand that his parents were on a tireless, never-ending quest to get and provide him with the absolute best care anyone could ever ask for.”

John Travolta paid tribute to his late friend Lisa Marie Presley

In the wake of the tragic passing of Lisa, Travolta, a longtime friend of the late star, paid a heartfelt tribute. 

Lisa’s sudden demise on January 12, 2023, following a cardiac arrest, left her loved ones devastated. The news was confirmed by her mother as she expressed, “It is with a heavy heart that I must share the devastating news that my beautiful daughter Lisa Marie has left us,” to People.

Amid this profound loss, the family requested privacy to cope with their grief, appreciating the outpouring of love and prayers they received.

Travolta, who shared a close bond with Lisa, expressed his deep sorrow at her untimely passing. Taking to Instagram the next day, January 13, 2023, he posted a poignant tribute to his dear friend. 

Travolta wrote, “Lisa baby girl, I’m so sorry. I’ll miss you but I know I’ll see you again,” alongside a picture of the actress who was 54 years old at the time of her death. 

Furthermore, the renowned actor extended his love and heartfelt condolences to Riley Keough, Lisa’s children, Priscilla, her mother, and Harper Vivienne Ann Lockwood and Finley Aaron Love Lockwood, her twin daughters.

Late Actor Lance Reddick Had Great Relationship with His Wife and Two Kids

Lance Reddick’s wife, Stephanie Reddick, is deeply saddened by his demise.

Late actor Lance Reddick and his wife, Stephanie Reddick, shared a strong and enduring relationship, built on love and trust, spanning over two decades.

Lance met his wife in 1999 while he was working on a play at the Guthrie Theater in Minneapolis, Minnesota, whereas Stephanie was an employee there. The two quickly became close and developed a romantic connection, leading to a decade-long relationship.

Later, the couple eventually tied the knot in 2011 in a low-key ceremony at Bar Lurcat in Minneapolis, Stephanie’s home state. They also deliberately chose a single venue for both the ceremony and reception to simplify things for out-of-town guests.

During the wedding, the actor set only two rules for the celebration. The first rule was that everyone had to have fun, and the second was that Lance couldn’t see his wife’s wedding dress before she walked down the aisle.

Similarly, Stephanie embraced simplicity in her wedding preparations, allocating a modest $250 for her dress, which the actor personally picked up from the post office. She also chose floral arrangements for the dress details.

Lance Reddick and his wife, Stephanie Reddick, were together for over two decades. (Source: Spamchronicles)

Recalling her relaxed attitude, she shared, “I told the florist I didn’t care about the flower type, as long as they were pretty. She suggested peonies because they were in season. I didn’t even know the flower color until the wedding day, and they turned out beautifully.”

Additionally, she fondly recalled her favorite moment as the day her mom saw her in the dress and moved to tears.

Although the couple openly shared details about their love story and wedding, they intentionally chose to keep the rest of their married life more private, valuing a sense of intimacy and personal space.

However, the actor and his wife made several red-carpet appearances together over the years, attending events such as premieres for movies like White House Down, The Blacklist, and Godzilla vs Kong.

Now, following Lance’s untimely passing due to heart disease, his wife took a moment to connect with her late husband’s fans and express profound gratitude. Using the late actor’s Twitter and Instagram accounts, Stephanie conveyed;

“Lance was taken from us far too soon. Thank you for all your overwhelming love, support, and beautiful stories shared on these platforms over the last day. I see your messages and can’t begin to express how grateful I am to have them.”

In addition to this heartfelt message, she extended special thanks to the thousands of Destiny players who participated in a tribute to Lance, emphasizing, “And to the thousands of Destiny players who played in a special tribute to Lance, thank you. Lance loved you as much as he loved the game.”

Upon seeing the post, an outpouring of condolences and tributes flooded in from the actor’s friends and fans. Halle Berry, a fellow star, shared a poignant comment, “My love and prayers are with the family at this unfortunate time. Lance was one of the most sincere people I’d ever met. Truly one of a kind! RIP sweet king.”

Likewise, Ana Gasteyer shared her condolences, stating, “Sending so much love and sympathy. He was an extraordinary talent and had an amazing career. And he loved his wife and family so much. A life, far too short, but so well lived.”

Lance Reddick has two children, but not with his wife Stephanie Reddick

Lance and his wife, Stephanie, didn’t have children together, although Stephanie likely played a key role in raising Lance’s two kids. The late actor had children with his first wife, Suzanne Yvonne Louis — a daughter named Yvonne Nicole and a son named Christopher Reddick.

As of now, both of his children have grown up. But as Lance deliberately kept them out of the spotlight throughout his life, he didn’t share many pictures of his kids on social media, and neither talked about being a father in any of his interviews.

Inside the marital life of Lance Reddick and his first wife Suzanne Yvonne Louis

Lance married his first wife, jewelry designer Suzanne, in 1986. The details of their initial meeting and the early days of their relationship remain shrouded in mystery.

Shortly after their wedding, the couple decided to part ways. Post-separation, Suzanne became a mother to Kaiya Dane Meier, her child with Karl Meier.

Unfortunately, the actor’s first wife faced a tragic turn of events, suffering a heart attack and falling into a coma from which she never recovered. Later, on November 3, 2011, she passed away, marking a deeply sorrowful chapter in their lives.

After Suzanne’s passing, Lance candidly discussed the profound loss of his first wife and the mother of his two children in an interview with Strombo in 2012.

Reflecting on the impact of the tragedy, the actor emphasized, “It made me realize that I always have to keep in front of me why I’m doing this.”

Lance Reddick with one of his two children (Photo: Facebook)

Sharing the emotional weight of becoming his kids’ sole parent, he stated, “I felt it more with my ex-wife because suddenly I was my kids’ only parent… I have great kids, and it’s because they had a great mom.”

After his first wife’s passing, Lance and his second wife, Stephanie, continued to care for and raise his children, providing them with love and support.

Melissa Fumero Had Crush on Husband David Fumero Long before Wedding — Their Married Life

Melissa Fumero and David Fumero have been together for more than a decade.

American actress Melissa Fumero had a crush on her now-husband, David Fumero, ever since her teenage years, and little did she know at the time that one day, he would become her spouse.

When Melissa had a crush on David, he was a young model who even made a brief cameo in Mariah Carey’s 1997 hit single ‘Honey.’ However, when the actress finally met David on the set of One Life to Live, she didn’t recognize him.

At that time, Melissa played the role of Adriana Cramer, while her now-beau appeared as Cristian Vega on the show. As the duo kept spending time together on the show, they formed a close bond and began dating, but she didn’t realize he was her childhood crush.

The surprise came later during a date when the Brooklyn Nine-Nine star’s husband mentioned the ‘Honey’ video. Suddenly, everything clicked for Melissa, and the revelation was so overwhelming that she had to excuse herself from the room.

After several years of dating, the couple decided to take their relationship to the next level and tied the knot on December 10, 2007. Their wedding ceremony was uniquely infused with delightful Christmas themes, adding a festive and memorable touch to their special day.

As of now, the lovebirds have been happily together for over a decade. On December 10, 2019, Melissa marked their 12th wedding anniversary and commemorated the occasion by sharing a cherished photo from their wedding day.

Likewise, in her heartfelt caption, she reflected on the 12 years of shared experiences — the good times, bad times, happy moments, sad moments, sexy moments, silly moments, and everything in between.

She also went on to express her hope to continue filling the next 12 years with just as many cherished memories with her husband. “Let’s see if we can fill up the next 12 years just as much @davidfumero. Happy Anniversary my love,” she wrote.

The actress ended her message with a sweet note, acknowledging her husband as her everything and playfully mentioning that the Instagram post served as his anniversary card since she forgot to get one.

Melissa Fumero with her husband, David Fumero, at the premiere of ‘The Vault’ (Photo: Instagram)

Upon seeing the post, one person left a comment saying, “You look great together… Happy to see you happy in life outside the series,” while another commenter described them as the “PERFECT COUPLE” and added a yellow heart emoji.

Who is Melissa Fumero’s husband? Brief on David Fumero’s career

David’s journey into the entertainment industry began in his early years when he first ventured into modeling during his youth. Surprisingly, acting wasn’t initially on his radar until a friend, who was actively taking acting classes, persuaded him to give it a try.

His first role was in the legendary daytime drama One Life To Live, where he stepped into the shoes of Cristian Vega, a character originally portrayed by actor Yorlin Madera from 1998 until 2011.

Subsequently, he expanded his repertoire to include various movies such as Carrie’s Choice, Greetings from the Shore, Manhattanites, Crazy Bitches, and more.

In addition to his film ventures, he has made notable appearances on television, featuring in well-known shows like Law & Order: Criminal Intent, Power, NCIS: Los Angeles, L.A.’s Finest, and others.

Melissa Fumero and David Fumero are blessed with two wonderful children

Melissa and David joyfully welcomed their first child, Enzo Fumero, into the world on March 24, 2016. Shortly after the heartwarming arrival, the actress shared a precious snapshot of her newborn on March 27, 2016, with the caption, “Sweetest face I’ve ever held. Enzo 3/24.”

The post garnered warm responses from followers, with one person expressing, “Aww, so cute baby!” and another noting, “What a beautiful baby! Look at all that hair! You are truly blessed!”

Similarly, approximately three years after welcoming their first child, Melissa revealed her second pregnancy on Instagram in November 2019. In a candid post featuring a mirror selfie showcasing her growing baby bump, she humorously wrote, “Oh yeah, I’m hella pregnant.”

Melissa Fumero and David Fumero have two sons. (Source: Instagram)

The actress then went on to open up about the challenges of this pregnancy, confessing, “To be honest, I haven’t felt like posting about it because this pregnancy has been way harder and… I don’t feel that cute?”

However, she shared a positive moment from the day before, describing an intense massage, an awesome chiropractic adjustment, and the feeling of thicker hair, which made her feel a little bit cute.

Melissa and her husband welcomed their second child, Axel Fumero, into the world on February 14, 2020. Just as they did with their firstborn, Melissa shared a heartwarming black-and-white photo of her newborn son on Instagram.

Her caption resonated with love and joy, stating, “Welcome to the world, Axel. You have made Valentine’s Day my new favorite holiday!”

Andy Samberg and His Wife Joanna Newsom Had a Beautiful Wedding Ceremony

Andy Samberg and his wife Joanna Newsom consistently put effort into their relationship.

American actor Andy Samberg and his wife Joanna Newsom had an enchanting yet super private wedding in the presence of their close friends and family members.

According to US Weekly, on September 21, 2013, Samberg and Newsom exchanged their vows at the picturesque Post Ranch Inn in Big Sur, California. Leading up to the special day, the actor had shared insights into the preparations, expressing a mix of stress and excitement.

He remarked, “So far, it’s been nothing but stress and joy. The joy is the lady herself. The stress is the planning. We tried to go small and it was just impossible. There are too many relatives, too many friends.”

Later, for the wedding, Newsom adorned herself in a stunning Zac Posen wedding gown, a breathtaking choice that added an extra layer of grace and beauty to the celebration.

Meanwhile, Samberg donned a bespoke suit crafted by the talented David Hart, ensuring that every detail of their attire reflected the elegance and style befitting such a special occasion.

Likewise, the star-studded guest list featured some of Hollywood’s brightest names, such as Amy Poehler, Nick Kroll, Maya Rudolph, Paul Thomas Anderson, Lorne Michaels, and Adam Sandler.

Even though the wedding was filled with laughter, there were moments of genuine emotion as well. According to a source, Samberg’s speech achieved the perfect balance between humor and sentiment, leading to tears from both him and his wife.

Moreover, the evening before the wedding, the joyful couple was also celebrated at a lively rehearsal dinner, where many of the comedically gifted guests entertained with stand-up performances.

After tying the knot, the actor humorously quipped to People stating, “I liked it and I put a ring on it.” He also shared details about their enchanting honeymoon in the Maldives, describing it as an incredible experience.

Similarly, Newsom later affectionately expressed that her husband is her “favorite person in the world,” praising him as the biggest superfan of her music. She continued, saying, “He’s the person I would most want to hang out with at any given moment.”

Andy Samberg and Joanna Newsom first met through mutual friends

Samberg and Newsom’s love story began in 2008 when they were introduced through mutual friends. The connection was instant, but what added a charming layer to their romance was the actor’s pre-meeting admiration for his future wife’s musical talents.

On the other hand, Newsom was also captivated by her husband’s acting talent. She confessed to being a fan of his work, particularly recalling the night they first met at one of her shows. Fred Armisen, a mutual acquaintance, brought Samberg along, and Newsom couldn’t help but appreciate his comedic talent.

She fondly remembered the actor’s cameo in The Lonely Island’s sketch video ‘Just 2 Guyz,’ where he played the character Steve the c**t. When they met, Newsom greeted him with a playful reference to his on-screen persona.

Just a few days after their initial meeting, the two embarked on a romantic journey together. Despite both being public figures, they chose to keep their love story away from the spotlight, providing space for the genuine connection they formed to flourish.

As they’re private about their personal life, they rarely make public appearances. Newsom doesn’t even use social media, and Samberg hasn’t featured his wife on his. Furthermore, Samberg and Newsom’s wedding pictures haven’t even been made public to this date.

Andy Samberg and Joanna Newsom are proud parents of a daughter

Samberg and Newsom’s representative confirmed to People in August 2017 that the married couple had joyfully welcomed their first child, a daughter, whose details are undisclosed. Embracing the challenges of first-time parenthood, the duo united to share the responsibilities.

Speaking to iNews in 2018, the actor emphasized the importance of leaning into the experience. He stated, “With a baby, it’s all about just sitting down and watching TV with my wife at the end of the day; Wild Wild Country or something.”

“That way, we can focus on everyone else’s problems. We try to find the humor in everything,” he added. Similarly, during the COVID-19 lockdown, Samberg and Newsom, like many others, took a break from their work engagements.

This pause allowed them to cherish family life and witness their daughter following in her mother’s footsteps. Likewise, in an interview with GQ, the actor expressed delight at their daughter emulating her mom, calling it the cutest thing ever.

Andy Samberg with his baby daughter in July 2018 (Photo: Twitter)

Humorously, he admitted they had become “those cliché people” during the lockdown, even watching Mad Men for the first time, despite his longtime friendship with the show’s star, Jon Hamm.

Additionally, in a 2021 interview with People, Samberg provided a glimpse into how they’ve perfected their parenting routine. He painted a picture of their daily life, filled with precious family moments like sitting in the sun and dancing in the kitchen.

Furthermore, Samberg shared that he and his wife, along with their adorable daughter, were actively involved in a small playgroup with other families.

He also detailed their daily rhythm, juggling work during the day and smoothly transitioning into dedicated family time in the evenings, including making dinner, bathing, going to bed, and finally, collapsing after a full day of parenting.

Who is Andy Samberg’s wife? Brief on her career

At the young age of four, Newsom started learning the piano, and by the time she was eight, she had already mastered the harp.

Joanna Newsom developed an interest in music since her childhood. (Source: Collapse Board)

She subsequently enrolled at Mills College in Oakland, California, where she delved into the realms of musical composition and creative writing. However, her academic path took a turn when she decided to drop out, as reported by the New York Times.

In a 2004 interview with The A.V. Club, Newsom shared that around eighth grade, she decided that she wanted to be a composer. So, that’s when her focus shifted again, and that’s what she went to college for.

After this transformative period, she evolved into a well-known singer and harpist, celebrated for her literary lyrics and distinctive sound. Newsom first released her EPs in 2002, marking the beginning of her musical career.

Shortly after, she signed with Drag City and made her debut with ‘The Milk-Eyed Mender’ in 2004. This marked the inception of her artistic journey, which further unfolded with the release of her fourth full-length album, Divers, in 2015.

Beyond her musical achievements, Newsom ventured into film and television. Notably, she featured in MGMT’s Kids music video in 2009, played a harpist in a 2012 episode of Portlandia, and took on a role in Paul Thomas Anderson’s Inherent Vice.

Furthermore, she once made a brief cameo in the finale of NBC’s (formerly Fox’s) police drama, Brooklyn Nine-Nine, in 2021. Her husband, Samberg, was one of the lead cast members — he played the role of Detective Jake Peralta.
