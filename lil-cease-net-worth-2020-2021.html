Early Life & Biography

Lil was born and brought up in Brooklyn, New York. He has a distinctive lisping style. From an early age, he developed a great interest in music. In 1994, when he was a teenager, he became a member of the famous rock band the “Junior M.A.F.I.A”. He made his debut in the year 1994 that issued the single “Players Anthem.” In the next year, he came up with a full-length Conspiracy and scored a hit single with the Lil’ Kim as “Crush on You.”

On March 9, 1997, while he was driving with the Notorious B.I.G., his fellow member Biggie was shot. He died at the moment. Due to this incident, Lil’ Cease turned into a solo artist. After signing to the Queen Bee label of Lil’ Kim, he issued “The Wonderful World of Cease A Leo” around 1999.

Career

In 1995, Lil launched his debut album, “Conspiracy,” with the band ‘Junior M.A.F.I.A.’ With his first album, he became so popular and gave a kickstart to his career. The single of this album, “Player’s Anthem,” obtained mainstream attention from people.

He was also featured on the soundtrack for the comedy movie “How to Be a Player” (1997). Later, he appeared on Lil’ Kim’s song “Crush on You” from the album “Hard Core.” This song reached the top position on the Hot Rap Songs chart.

This album “The Wonderful World of Cease A Leo” (1999) peaked at 26th position on the Billboard 200.

In January 2009, he released “Letter to B.I.G.” from the recording to the movie “Notorious.”
