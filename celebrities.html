Niall Horan News - Us Weekly

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, February 9, 2024

Name:Niall Horan

Relationship:Single

Birthday:September 13, 1993

About

Born September 13, 1993, in Mullingar, County Westmeath, Ireland, Niall Horan is one-fifth of the European boy band One Direction.

Simon Cowell created One Direction during The X Factor in 2010; the other band members are Zayn Malik, Liam Payne, Harry Styles and Louis Tomlinson. The group finished in third place but signed a recording contract with Cowell’s Syco Records.

The band experienced worldwide success with the 2011 release of their debut album, Up All Night. They released their sophomore effort, Take Me Home, the following year.
